One year ago, on May 31, 2024, the very first public commit to AliasVault was made [on GitHub](https://github.com/lanedirt/AliasVault/commit/0a3d35c603218d6a2dc030018a1e84e7030946b8). What started as a simple prototype for a privacy-first alias and credential manager has grown into a full-featured, cross-platform tool that helps users take back control of their digital identities.
